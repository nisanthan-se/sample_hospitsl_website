Our History

Over 50 years of dedicated healthcare service to our community. From our humble beginnings to becoming a leading medical institution, our commitment to patient care has never wavered.

50+ Years of Service
200+ Medical Professionals
500+ Hospital Beds
1M+ Patients Treated

1970 - Foundation

City Hospital was founded by Dr. Robert Johnson with a vision to provide quality healthcare to the growing community. Starting with just 20 beds and 5 physicians, we began our journey of healing and care.

1985 - Expansion

We expanded our facilities to include a new surgical wing and maternity ward, increasing our capacity to 100 beds. This expansion allowed us to serve more patients with specialized care.

1995 - Technology Advancement

City Hospital became one of the first in the region to implement electronic medical records and advanced diagnostic equipment, revolutionizing patient care and treatment accuracy.

2005 - Cardiac Center

We established a state-of-the-art Cardiac Center, offering advanced heart care services including cardiac surgery, angioplasty, and comprehensive rehabilitation programs.

2015 - Research Institute

The City Hospital Research Institute was founded, focusing on innovative treatments for chronic diseases and collaborating with international medical research centers.

2020 - Pandemic Response

During the global health crisis, City Hospital served as a regional response center, treating over 5,000 patients and implementing innovative telehealth services that continue today.

2024 - Present Day

Today, City Hospital stands as a 500-bed facility with over 200 specialized physicians, offering comprehensive healthcare services and continuing our legacy of excellence in patient care.
